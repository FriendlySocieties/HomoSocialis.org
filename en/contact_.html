We are currently working on preparations for the first trial project. It is about voluntary participants of a specific region. In the interest of protecting their privacy, we will not disclose any information before the start of the first phase.

In the case that you have a background in a scientific field (sociology, economy) and have interest in accompaniment, research, or analysis, then you are warmly welcomed to come participate in this experiment. Please write us in this case starting in 2020.

Our focus in the first phase lies in covering emergencies and not in a type of self-governing universal decentralized welfare system for health, education, retirement, etc. Our texts, homepage, and upcoming software parts are open-source and we are excited about all who work on similar projects. We will provide a platform to exchange experiences.
