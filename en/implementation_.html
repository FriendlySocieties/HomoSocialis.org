Implementation:

The technical implementation occurs for example through the use of Bitcoin as a medium of exchange and Bitcoin Smart Contracts over Rootstock (RSK) with a parallel own DAO and Blockchain implemented in Scala with Scorex as a framework. For programming enthusiasts, there is currently a large field of possibilities and also persuasions.

In just 6 months, the blockchain landscape will have already drastically changed and further developed itself. Furthermore, we only expect a project start with the first participants in 1-2 years, and then a 1-2 year experiment phase, probably with a few adjustment due to gained experiences.

For this reason, we are working on a still centralized prototype for the experiment phase .

Our focus lies in the network consisting of people, the dynamics which are developed therein and in which form and under which rules the goal of mutual coverage for emergencies can be achieved most reasonably.

All from us developed software will be published at no cost as an open source.
